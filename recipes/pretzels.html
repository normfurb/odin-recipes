Pretzels

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 package yeast
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/4 cup baking soda
  • 3/4 coarse salt

Steps

  1. Warm milk and butter, add brown sugar and stir.
  2. In a seperate bowl mix flour, yeast and salt, then slowly add warm milk.
  3. Knead until mixture until dough is smooth and let set for 1-2 hrs.
